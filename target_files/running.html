Running

In my free time I'm a keen runner and have competed in several ultra-marathons. These include the Winter Spine Race, the Hardmoors 200 and the Arc of Attrition.

Most of my runs can be found on Strava

I've been plotting my more interesting (and longer runs) on a map.

During the Summer of 2022 I (accidentally) entered two 100 mile races a week apart. These were the Peak District 100 and South Downs 100

At part of my training for the line I solo fastpacked along Hadrian's Wall

In the summer of 2023 I successfully completed the line race.
