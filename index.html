Employment

I currently work as a Senior Software Developer for a startup called ZeroMission. which creates software for EV Fleet management. We use a range of technolgies which include C#, Postgres, AWS, and React.js.

Past Employment

For several years I was employed by the Proactis Group, developing e-procurement software. During this time I provided solutions for a range of global companies including universities, large charities, city banks, and housing trusts.

After Proactis, I joined a very small startup called Hivemap which researched and developed predictive planning software for construction projects.



York Developers

I am a co-founder of the York Developers charity, which arranges technical meetups and events in York. These include a monthly Code Dojo where attendees pair up and work on a new problem each month.

I have spoken a number of times at community usergroups on a range of topics from 'Type Systems in Languages' to the internals of 'Microsoft SQL Server'


Sport

In my free time I'm a keen runner and have competed in several ultra-marathons. These include 'The Line', the Winter Spine Race, the Hardmoors 200 and the Arc of Attrition. More information about my running can be found here.
